Risk & Pause Policy

Capital protection is prioritized during abnormal market conditions.

During geopolitical events, abnormal volatility, market crashes, major news events, or unstable liquidity conditions, XTech may pause bot activity to reduce avoidable exposure.

If active trades need to be closed during a pause, a small realized loss may occur depending on the current running loss value at that time. The objective is to reduce larger risk and protect long-term trading stability.

Capital Withdrawal Terms

Important explanation before recovering investment capital.

Algorithmic trading systems may keep open trades during normal operation. At any moment, some trades may show running profit and some may show running loss. Trades usually close when the bot-defined target condition is met.

If the client decides to withdraw capital while trades are open, the final recovered amount may be affected by the current running loss or open trade status visible in MT4. Therefore, withdrawal timing should be reviewed carefully before action.
